Mathematical Operations

#26/06/2025 #mathematics

Addition

$\begin{array}{r} A\,\,\, \\\\+ B\,\,\, \\\\ \overline{\,\,\,C\,\,\,} \\\\ \end{array}$ or $A + B = C$

  • $A$ and $B$ = Addends
  • $C$ = Sum or Total

Subtraction

$\begin{array}{r} M\,\, \\\\- S\,\,\, \\\\ \overline{\,\,\,R\,\,\,} \\\\ \end{array}$ or $M - S = R$

  • $M$ = Minuend
  • $S$ = Subtrahend
  • $R$ = Remainder

Multiplication

$\begin{array}{r} A\,\,\, \\\\ \times B\,\,\, \\\\ \overline{\,\,\,C\,\,\,} \\\\ \end{array}$ or $A \times B = C$

  • $A$ = Multiplicand
  • $B$ = Multiplier
  • $C$ = Product or Total
Note:
The numbers $A$ and $B$ can also be called Factors.

Division

$\begin{array}{l} D\, | \underline{\,\,\,\,\, d \,\,\,\,\,} \\ R\, \,\,\,\,\,\, Q \end{array}$

  • $D$ = Dividend
  • $d$ = Divisor
  • $R$ = Remainder
  • $Q$ = Quotient
